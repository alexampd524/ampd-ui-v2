descriptionNo Release Documents
i
Released vehicles where the required classified document is missing or was uploaded under a generic type. Each disposition requires a specific doc — Signed Transport Release for transporter handoffs, Redemption Agreement for customer redemptions, Voluntary Release for vol repos, Condition Report Acknowledgement for auction and client deliveries. Generic uploads (e.g. “Document”, “Other”) do not satisfy this requirement. Clients use these to approve payment — wrong or missing doc types are a common cause of invoice disputes and delayed collections.

receipt_longNo PP Receipt on File
i
Cases with a personal property release recorded but no Signed Personal Property Receipt uploaded as a classified document. A signed receipt is required as proof the debtor acknowledged and retrieved their belongings. Without it the case is out of compliance — agents cannot demonstrate the release occurred, which creates legal exposure. Generic uploads do not satisfy this requirement. The document must be uploaded specifically as a Personal Property Receipt.
